Featured Project

Solar analysis and shadow simulation tool featured at IIT Kharagpur

SolAxis - Daylight & Sun Path Analyzer


SolAxis is a scientific desktop application that calculates solar altitude, azimuth, daylight duration, and shadow projections based on geographic location, date, and object height. It provides interactive visualizations and exports analytical data for architectural planning and solar studies. The tool was recognized and featured at IIT Kharagpur for its practical engineering application and accurate solar modeling.
